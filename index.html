About Me

Hello world! My name is David Sanchez and I am 25 years old. I am currently a student attending the University of Miami. In 2017 I graduated from the College of Charleston with a major in International Business, and minor in Spanish. I chose to attend the Full-Stack Web Development program because I know that learning code will have a positive impact on my professional future, and I am excited to embark on this web development journey. Around 6 months ago I found myself pondering on where I would be in life in about 1-2 years. I realized that even though I had a Bachelor’s Degree in a field I was interested in, and was

full of jobs, I noticed that the path I was walking down was not one I ultimately wanted to continue on. I figured that I needed something else to supplement my degree that would allow me to use my creative intelligence just as much as my critical intelligence. I came to the conclusion that Web Development would provide for me the opportunity to create, and use my brain in a way I knew I was more than capable of doing, however, I knew embarking on this Web Development journey would be one filled with many challenges. My whole life I have always been ready to hop on to the next adventure, and even though my ambition has served me well, I believe that it has also presented me with an innate unwillingness to sit still. In this 6-month long course I will need to have patience with myself and know that if I try hard enough, and don’t give up, I will be able to succeed. If I’m persistent in completing an objective, or project, I know that the feeling of accomplishment will have been worth the patience I put in. Having patience will also be important because even though I like things to be done instantly, I’m aware that 6-months will not feel instantaneous. Even though being patient with myself will be a challenge at times, I’m confident I will be able to use this virtue to ultimately aid me in my success, not just with this course, but with my life as a whole.
